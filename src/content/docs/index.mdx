<span class="section-label">A DAY WITH THINKUBE</span>

## How your day changes.

**Morning.** *Without Thinkube: SSH into the VM, `docker exec` into the Jupyter container, find yesterday's notebook on the bind mount, hope the GPU passthrough still works.* With Thinkube: Open JupyterLab in your browser. Tell Thinky: "Load yesterday's experiment from MLflow and try a different embedding model." It writes the code, runs it, compares the results. You drink your coffee.

**Afternoon.** *Without Thinkube: Add to compose.yml, configure Traefik labels, SSH in and pull. No CI/CD — you are the pipeline. And when you need to scale, you start over.* With Thinkube: Claude Code helps you build a FastAPI backend. Push to Git. Your app builds, deploys, and is live at `myapp.yourdomain.com` with TLS before you finish lunch.

**Evening.** *Without Thinkube: Download model weights, write a serving script, configure GPU memory, set up a reverse proxy, hope nothing conflicts.* With Thinkube: Deploy a new Llama model through the dashboard. One click. It loads onto your GPU and joins your LLM Gateway. Your agents use it immediately.
